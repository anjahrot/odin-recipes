Recipe for a delicious tzatziki

Description

Tzatziki is a dressing from Greece and Turkey. It tastes delicious on your Greek salad, pita, grilled veggies, and more

Ingredients:

  • Cucumber
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Greek youghurt
  • Spices and herbs

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Grate the cucumber (use largest holes on your grater)
  2. Squeeze water out of the grated cucumber
  3. Stir everything together
