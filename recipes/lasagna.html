Recipe for the best lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
